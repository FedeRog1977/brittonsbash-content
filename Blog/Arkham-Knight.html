Arkham Knight

It’s not the greatest game of all time but it certainly puts across some of the most important messages of crime, punishment and the management of people surrounding these issues. The game uses characters, story-telling methods, themes and environments exceptionally well to portray fear, integrity, stability, objectivity, and completionism. This is why it is more than just a game. It’s a story of how to be the greatest of all time and use it in the best possible way.
