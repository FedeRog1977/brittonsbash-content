Like all the normies, in 2014 I fell into the trap of creating an Instagram account. Sure it’s brought me some very minor joy and pleasure as I scroll endlessly looking at Wilson’s latest releases on the TennisWarehouse page but, get a grip Lewis. I’m not one of those idiots who ‘loses control’ and ‘can’t stop’ but if I ever do choose to have a scroll, I can’t help thinking it’s the biggest waste of time possible. Real ‘grammable’ moments from my life will now appear here.
