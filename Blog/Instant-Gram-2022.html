    Four Different Kind Hol-e-day | 7th-14th of June

    A four different kind holiday with rice stick (rice pasta). Round II of cancelled sleazyJet flights this time meant it was a two-day stop at the Premier Inn in the fine Wilmslow, on easyJet of course. We filled this with plenty of Coach & Four, Mei Dim rice sticks, Le Guy D’Familia, Papa D’Americano, Giada and Juniper, again of course on easyJet. Munich for one night but still plenty of time to inhale a fat schnitzel and take in the Hofbräuhaus for a few beer, large. Good seven-hour stunner of a coach journey the next day down to Verona, my personal favourite of the locations, where we inhaled some magnificent pasta and wild bore ragù followed by a five-star cocktaileria. Some stunning strolls, gelaterias, beererias, farinerias, cannolerias, casinoroyalerias, celinerias, bradoerias, topped off with one stunning restauranteria on the final night. Greatly enjoyable 5:30 rise for 14 hours of travelling the next day.
