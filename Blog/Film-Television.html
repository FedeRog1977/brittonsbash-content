Here, you’ll find the full comprehensive list of all of my favourite films and television shows over the period of 1960 to the present. Of course, on a fundamental level I tend to favour Touchstone Pictures and Paramount Pictures, especially at the John Hughes and Michael Mann unreachable pinnacles, but this list does reach a little beyond. Films and TV shows are listed from favourite to least favourite according to each year.
