These recipes are my daily drivers and I take great pride in perfecting these to my taste. I have typical weekday recipes for function (i.e. the all-5,000-grams-of-carbs diet to fuel the hills) and, more expansive weekend recipes for enjoyment. The first is based on minimising ingredients and costs. I like to rely on consistently cheap and functional ingredients, not the inconsistency of ‘special offers’. I aim to consume relatively similar foods during the week for effective economies of scale. I tend to spend approximately £20 per week on weekday ingredients. The second is based on more complex ingredients, methods and flavours; using a greater number of ingrediateds and generally more time. My budget for these tends to also be approximately £20.
