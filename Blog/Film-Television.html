Here, you’ll find comprehensive lists of all of my favourite films, television shows and music over the period of 1960 to the present. Also, a general summary of my favourite media. Of course, on a fundamental level I tend to favour Touchstone Pictures and Paramount Pictures, especially at the John Hughes and Michael Mann unreachable pinnacles, but this list does reach a little beyond. Everything is listed from favourite to least favourite; according to each year in the context of the comprehensive lists.
