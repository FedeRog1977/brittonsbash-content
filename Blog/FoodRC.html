BrittonsFoodRC

This blog entry explores recipes practiced and developed by myself. These recipes are my daily drivers and I take great pride in perfecting these to my taste. My cooking follows two frameworks: [1] the typical weekday recipes which I use for function and [2], more expansive weekend recipes I use for enjoyment. The former is based on minimising ingredients, and therefore minimsing fixed costs; I like to rely on consistently cheap and functional ingredients, not the inconsistency of ‘special offers’. I aim to consure relatively similar foods during the week to allow ingredients to work efficiently and be incorporated into as many different recipes as possible. I tend to spend approximately £20 per week on weekday ingredients. The latter is based on more complex ingredients, methods and flavours; using a greater number of ingrediateds and generally more time. My budget for these tends to also be approximately £20. These are primarily what are included on this page as they are more aesthetically pleasing. Some recipes are also included here.
